Welcome to Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church Resources

Who We Are

We are a dedicated community of believers, passionate about sharing the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo faith with the world. Our mission is to provide a digital space where EOTC material is easily accessible. Whether you are a lifelong member of our church or just starting to explore our traditions, this website is for you.

What We Offer

Kidase (ስርዓተ ቅዳሴ): Dive into the spiritual depth of Kidase, a sacred liturgy that has been passed down through generations.

All Deacon Parts: Explore the various Deacon components of our worship services, each contributing to the tapestry of our faith.

Misbaks (ምስባክ): Delve into and learn the beautiful weekly Misbaks that inspire our devotion.

Wudase Mariam (ውዳሴ ማርያም): Experience the devotion and spirituality of Wudase Mariam, a cornerstone of our faith.

Tselot Zezewetir (ጸሎት ዘዘወትር): Uncover the intricate traditions of Tselot Zezewetir, a testament to our faith's strength.

Mezmur of the Day: Enjoy the soothing melodies and soulful messages of our Mezmur, shared daily.

Thank you for visiting. We hope you find inspiration, knowledge, and a deeper connection to our faith on this website. Explore and discover, for the journey has just begun.
